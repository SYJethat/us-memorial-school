About Our School

स्वर्गीय उदयभान शत्रुघ्न मिश्र सेवा संस्थान

U S Memorial Public School is a premier educational institution established under the visionary guidance of Swaragya Udaybhan Shatrughn Mishra Seva Sansthan (Registered). Located in the heart of Newada Jhumila, Gorakhpur, our school has been dedicated to providing quality education since its inception.

We believe in nurturing young minds through a perfect blend of academic excellence and character building. Our English medium education from Nursery to 8th standard ensures that every child receives the best possible foundation for their future academic journey.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality education to all students regardless of their background. We strive to create a learning environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Our mission is to develop responsible citizens who contribute positively to society while achieving their personal goals.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that transforms young minds into confident, capable, and compassionate individuals. We envision creating a generation that bridges tradition with modernity, respecting Indian values while embracing global perspectives. Our goal is to make every child proud of their heritage while being prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
