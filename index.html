Welcome to U S Memorial Public School

U S Memorial Public School is a premier educational institution located in Newada Jhumila, Gorakhpur. Established under the visionary guidance of Swaragya Udaybhan Shatrughn Mishra Seva Sansthan (Registered), we are committed to providing quality education from Nursery to 8th standard.

Our English medium education ensures that every child receives the best possible foundation for their future academic journey. We blend traditional Indian values with modern teaching methodologies to create an environment where students can thrive academically and personally.
