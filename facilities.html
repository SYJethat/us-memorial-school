Infrastructure Details

Library

Our well-stocked library is a treasure house of knowledge with books for all age groups. From colorful picture books for tiny tots to reference materials for upper primary students, we have everything to spark the reading habit in children.

  • Over 1000 books
  • Story books in English and Hindi
  • Textbooks and reference materials
  • Newspaper and magazine section

Computer Lab

In today's digital age, computer literacy is essential. Our modern computer lab is equipped with the latest systems to provide hands-on experience to students. From basic typing to introductory programming, we cover all aspects of digital education.

  • Latest computer systems
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Educational software and learning apps
  • Trained computer instructors

Playground & Sports

Physical education is as important as academic learning. Our spacious playground provides ample space for various sports and outdoor activities. We encourage students to participate in sports for their physical fitness and team-building skills.

  • Large playing field
  • Indoor games room
  • Annual sports day
  • Trained sports instructors

Smart Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to provide a comfortable and conducive learning environment. Well-ventilated, spacious, and equipped with proper lighting, our classrooms ensure that students can focus on their studies without any discomfort.

  • Air circulation and ventilation
  • Proper lighting system
  • Comfortable seating arrangements
  • Green board and teaching aids

Safety & Security

The safety of our students is our top priority. We have implemented comprehensive security measures to ensure a safe learning environment for all children.

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance
  • Trained security personnel
  • Fire safety equipment
  • First aid facilities
